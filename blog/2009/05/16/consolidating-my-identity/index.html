Consolidating My Identity

May 16th, 2009

I like OpenID and have wanted to use it in my day-to-day internet usage. Up until recently I was attempting to use my @gmail.com account but now I have something even better.

The @gmail.com account was OK, but Google’s choice of login URI is weird and impossible to remember. Most OpenID services, myOpenID for instance, give you a URI in the pattern of “username.myopenid.com” to log in with. Easy to remember and, since most login forms let you forgo the https:// part it’s pretty natural to log in.

I took it one better though, my login URI is brendanjerwin.github.com. Now, GitHub is not an OpenID provider, but I’m still able to do this via a neat feature called “delegation.”
